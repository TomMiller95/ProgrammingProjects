This is my work history page. I have been working at MKS Inc. since June 2013, and while at CCC, I also tutored in Computer Science for a semester. Below are some images and descriptions that explain how some of my work was done.


MKS Inc.

How it Works

Each piece has a tail, which electricity runs through and lights up the lamp when it reacts with the ink. The way we get the electricity to connect is with terminals. I crimp the metal terminals on the tail(Shown above) and the silver part of the tail is conductive.

 How They are Attached

These are the terminals, which are all on a reel to start. I use a machine to crimp each piece at a time.





 How We Customize Each Piece

We make custom parts for every customer. This means we have to have a way to make the ink designs in any possible shape. To do this, we have our engineer design the designs on polyester(shown above) and we burn that design into an emulsion screen. This is done for every color on each image. Then we put ink on the areas burned.

 The Final Product

This is what one of our final products looks like. This one does not glow however, but it shows how we are able to put different colors in very specific areas in order to make each customer get their product exactly as they order it.
